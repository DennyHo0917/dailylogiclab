Star Battle Puzzle

Star Battle Puzzle Rules and Online Practice

Star Battle is a grid logic puzzle where the goal is to place stars without guessing. Each row, each column, and each outlined or colored region must contain exactly one star. Stars may not touch one another, including diagonally.

How to Play

  1. Look for a row, column, or region with very few possible star positions.
  2. Place a star only when that position is forced by the rules.
  3. Mark neighboring squares as impossible after placing a star.
  4. Keep checking rows, columns, and color regions until all stars are placed.

Beginner Strategy

Start with the smallest or most constrained color regions. If a region has only one square that can still hold a star, that square is forced. Once a star is placed, it eliminates its row, column, region, and all touching squares.
