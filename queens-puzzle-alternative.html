Queens Puzzle Alternative

Play a Queens-Style Star Battle Puzzle

If you like queens-style placement puzzles, Star Battle is a natural next puzzle to try. The goal is still to place one piece in every row, column, and region, but Daily Logic Lab uses stars and a no-touch rule: no two stars can touch, including diagonally.

Why Try Star Battle?

  • It is quick enough for a daily puzzle habit.
  • It has clear logic rules and does not require guessing.
  • Practice mode gives you more than one puzzle per day.
  • Every generated board is checked for exactly one solution.

Queens vs Star Battle

Queens-style puzzles and Star Battle both use row, column, and region constraints. Star Battle adds the no-touch rule, which makes neighboring cells important and creates a different solving rhythm.
