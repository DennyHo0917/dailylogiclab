Two Not Touch Puzzle Online

A two-not-touch puzzle is a short placement puzzle where pieces cannot touch each other. Daily Logic Lab uses the Star Battle version: place seven stars on a 7x7 board so each row, column, and color region has exactly one star.

Why Players Search for Two Not Touch

Many players enjoy the simple rule: no two stars may touch, even diagonally. The puzzle is quick to learn, easy to play on mobile, and deep enough to solve with logic instead of trial and error.

How This Version Works

  • One star in every row.
  • One star in every column.
  • One star in every color region.
  • No touching stars, including corners.
  • Every generated board is solver-verified for a unique solution.
